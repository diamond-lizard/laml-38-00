Time Out

Notes

Possibly the DBQ's most famous album, this contains Take Five, the most famous jazz track of that period. These experiments in different time signatures are what Dave Brubeck is most remembered for. Recorded Jun-Aug 1959 in NYC. See also the sequel, Time Further Out.
