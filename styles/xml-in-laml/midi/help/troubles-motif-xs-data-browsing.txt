IN CASE OF PROBLEMS WITH MOTIF XS VOICE OR ARP BROWSING:

Is the PC and the Motif XS connected by USB cable?

Instrument: 
  Is the instrument in Song Mode?
  Is the Arpeggio On/Off button in off position?

Instrument: Does the instrument receive from the USB port:
  Util > Control Midi: 
    Midi In/Out: USB
    Bank Select: On
    Program Change: On 
    Local Control: On (?)

PC: Is Caps Lock unselected?

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

NB: It is not necessary to select instrument nor MIDI output port when using
the voice or arp browser.
