LAML with DrScheme

DrScheme is the protected, user-friendly programming environment for PLT Scheme. DrScheme uses MzScheme internally.

DrScheme is too slow for LAML purposes, at least for my taste. And it is too difficult to activate LAML scripts. But, in principle, DRScheme works with LAML if you have time to wait.

There are special rules and advice for applying LAML with DrScheme.

Version 27: A sample XML-in-LAML file has been tested. OK. But not recommended for real-life LAML work.
